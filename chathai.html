Virtual Environment Design of a Tea Shop

Description & Inspiration


"Cha Thai" is a popular milk tea shop in Bristol. Creating a virtual world that captures the architectural and cultural character of "Cha Thai" was the project's main objective. The store was chosen because of its unusual interior design that combines modern and traditional Thai elements, offering special modeling and texturing challenges.

Responsibilities & Related Skills

1. Created a realistic virtual tea shop in Maya, referencing real-world images taken in Bristol.
2. Modelled detailed furniture, decorations and plants, applying textures and designing lighting of the environment.
