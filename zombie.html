VR Zombie Survival Shooting Game - Project Karlheinz

Description


This is a horror survival first-person shooting game . The story begins with our character getting injured during their initial entry into the Karlheinz facility. Left behind in the facility's stockroom, the character remains there until the leader of Team 2 arrives to assist and regroup. Upon discovering that the mother virus is located within the facility, the team realizes they must do whatever it takes to eradicate it. With this new mission, our character sets out to locate and destroy the mother virus once and for all.

In the game, the player will become an agent trying to escape and purify the asylum that is occupied by zombies. Aside from the regular zombie, the player also needs to face the giant boss zombie which is faster and stronger. During the development process, a lot of emphasis was put into the details, including the tutorial, setting of locomotion, mechanism of zombies, health, and weapon system in the game.


Inspiration


This game draws inspiration from SUPERHOT, known for its unique mechanic where time only moves when the player does. Players can strategically plan their actions in limited time, dodging bullets and defeating enemies, creating a tense blend of strategy and action. We drew on SUPERHOT's influence by focusing on time manipulation and strategic action, set limited time for teleportation, which aims to offer players similar tactical challenges while introducing our own unique story and world setting.

Responsibilities & Related Skills

1. Designed and developed the first-person game in Unity (C#) with teammates via GitLab for version control.
2. Implemented the interactive game tutorial and UI, guiding players through core mechanics while maintaining an intuitive user experience.
3. Created dynamic VR hand animations, poses, and trigger logic for immersive interactions and engagement.
