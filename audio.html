VR Planets Interactive Experience - Symphonic Planets

Description


In Symphonic Planets, players are planet builders who travel the universe shaping planets and harmonising worlds with unique elements (including fire, water, wood, ground, stone and thunder). Using gesture-based controls , players can move freely, gathering element balls drifting through the galaxy to craft their main planets. Players can also generate diverse terrains , all while the main planet's soundtrack evolves with each added element. This VR experience allows players interact with these element balls, shaping both the planet's appearance and its soundtrack. By the end, each player will have crafted a unique world and its accompanying soundtrack, blending the senses of sight and sound in a creative, immersive journey.

Responsibilities & Related Skills

1. Designed the realistic materials, particle effects of the element balls and set terrain generation in the main planet according to different elements in the game.
2. Used different instruments to compose the soundtracks based on the traits of the element balls in the game.
