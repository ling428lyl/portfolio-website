Virtual Environment Design: Exterior of a Bubble Milk Tea Shop

Description

This project presents the exterior design and modelling of the VR game Chill Cup. Developed in Maya, the model captures a playful, cartoon-inspired aesthetic through soft pastel tones, simplified forms and an oversized rooftop drink sculpture that reinforces the shop’s identity. The work focuses on architectural elements such as the rooftop, signage, props and day & night lighting. This phase concentrates solely on the exterior. Future development will expand into interior design and interactive gameplay for the VR experience.

Responsibilities & Related Skills

1. Created a cartoon-style milk tea shop exterior in Maya, with a modular layout designed for future interaction.
2. Modelled the building, decorative elements and plants, designed textures and applied materials.
3. Set up day and night scenes using Arnold area and mesh lights.
